## Executive Summary

This dossier evaluates three time-series databases for IoT telemetry storage.

**Recommendation:** InfluxDB 3.0 is the clear winner for modern IoT workloads.

## Recommendations

- **InfluxDB 3.0**: purpose-built for time-series, unmatched ingest performance, easy to operate.
- **TimescaleDB**: good if you need SQL, but adds PostgreSQL overhead.
- **QuestDB**: interesting but niche.

# Dossier: Key Facts Box — Missing

## Management Summary

This dossier skips directly to the Management Summary without a Key Facts box.
A reader who opens the dossier expecting a five-minute scan has no Key Facts table to reference.

The section-order may be fine, but the Key Facts box is absent, which is what this
fixture is designed to trigger.

## Key Concepts

| Term | What it is |
|------|-----------|
| **Key Facts** | A mandatory table near the top of every dossier |
